Items | Inventory
> Right click to equip and un-equip items. You can drag items within your inventory. When a merchant npc is active, right click the item to see the item value / sell.
Bank
> The Bank is for storing items and gold. The Bank is shared across all your characters!
Targeting | Top Left Corner
> Left click to target monsters and players. Press the character name between HP and MP bars to target yourself. You can see the properties/items of the targeted entity. You can only target one entity at a time.
Upgrades
> Find the NPC that offers upgrades and compounds in New Town, nearby, there is an NPC that sells scrolls. Weapons, Armors start at +0 and go all the way up to +10. +10 is almost impossible. Upgrade items by using upgrade scrolls, there are 3 grades of items, Normal, High and Rare, at first, you will only need the "Upgrade Scroll" that costs 1,000 gold.
Compounds
> For accessories, reaching a higher level is more challenging, for an +1 accessory, you need 3x +0 accessories of the same kind, and so on.
> You can use "Primordial Shard"s increase the chances of success of both upgrades and compounds, the essence of the shard is absorbed inside the item.

CODE
> You don't have to be a coder to use the CODE, you don't have to use the CODE to play Adventure Land, but CODE is the highlight of our game, so use it, try to learn a bit of coding too, it can be very fun and it's very useful
Attributes
Strength: Increases HP, Armor.
Intelligence: Increases MP, Resistance.
Dexterity: Increases the attack speed, run speed.
Vitality: Increases HP proportional to level.
Armor: 100 Armor reduces incoming physical damage by 10%.
Resistance: 100 Resistance reduces incoming magical damage by 10%.
Damage: Proportionally increases the attack.
Range: Increases the attack range.
Speed: Increases the run speed.
Stat: Becomes a main attribute with a scroll.
Evasion: Evade physical attacks.
Reflection: Reflect magical attacks back.
A.Piercing: Pierce through armor.
R.Piercing: Pierce through resistance.
Damage Return: Returns melee damage.
Abilities
> Abilities are currently in development. You can use existing abilities via their hotkeys or through CODE.
Mage's Mana Burst: Converts approximately half of the mana to damage.
Warrior's Charge: Increases speed for approximately 3 seconds.
Warrior's Taunt: Taunts the enemy with a higher range [Q]
Rogue's Invis: Goes invisible and deals 1.25X sneak damage
Rogue's Poisonous Touch: Poison reduces healing and attack speed
Ranger's Supershot: Has an extreme range, deals 1.5X damage
Priest's Curse: Slows down the enemy, amplifies damage taken, reduces attack
Limits
> You can play the game with 3 characters and one merchant simultaneously. These limits are IP based too. So if you are in the same house with multiple players, you'll need to share the allocation. Working on a system to bypass IP limitations for Steam and Mac clients.
Servers
> You can connect to any server you want. Your character is uniform across all servers. If you suddenly get disconnected, It's likely because the server is updating. You can usually re-connect in 2-3 minutes.
