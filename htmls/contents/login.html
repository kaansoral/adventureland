About Adventure Land
Adventure Land is an experimental Indie MMORPG. Anyone can CODE up to 4! characters, let Javascript do the grinding, while you do something productive!
Gameplay has many aspects to it. It's NON-P2W. You can UPGRADE weapons, COMBINE accessories, EXCHANGE rewards, CRAFT items, farm GOLD.
There are PVP maps with increased LUCK, GOLD, XP.
The ECONOMY is completely PEER-TO-PEER. You can keep your MERCHANT open 24/7 to SELL or BUY stuff.
There are a lot of planned features like GUILDS and DAILY EVENTS, they are entirely shaped by our community and their feedback!
